Common Questions

What is the typical funding timeline?
Most applications receive a decision within 24 hours. Funding is typically completed within 24-72 hours of final approval, depending on the complexity of the file.

What documentation is required?
For initial approval, we typically require just a completed application and your three most recent business bank statements. Additional documentation may be requested for larger loan amounts.

Will this affect my credit?
Our initial application involves a soft credit pull, which does not impact your credit score. Hard inquiries are generally only performed at the final stages of funding for specific products.
